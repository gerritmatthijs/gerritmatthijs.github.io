Welcome

I am currently a PhD student at the analysis department of the TU Delft, under the supervision of Martijn Caspers. My research is focused on operator algebras and noncommutative harmonic analysis. I am specifically interested in the theory beyond tracial von Neumann algebras and connections to Tomita-Takesaki modular theory.
