Welcome

As of October 2023 I am no longer working in academia, and starting working as a software engineer instead. This site will be kept online for reference purposes.
I used to be a PhD student at the analysis department of the TU Delft, under the supervision of Martijn Caspers. My research was focused on operator algebras and noncommutative harmonic analysis. More specifically, I researched the theory beyond tracial von Neumann algebras and connections to Tomita-Takesaki modular theory.
